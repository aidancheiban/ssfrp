About the Program

Students become part of real scientific research.

The Secondary School Field Research Program gives students the opportunity to work with mentors, collect field data, analyze results, and contribute to research connected to Earth and environmental science.

Program Highlights

What students experience

Field Research

Students participate in hands-on research projects involving real data, outdoor fieldwork, and scientific methods.

Mentorship

Students work with mentors and team leads who guide them through the research process.

Final Presentation

Students communicate their findings through presentations, visuals, and research summaries.
