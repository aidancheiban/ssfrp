Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory

Apply to the Program

SSFRP is a summer research program for high school students in the New York City area. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis each spring.

Open Positions

Choose the role that fits your experience.

Mentorship + project support

Team Lead

For college students ready to guide a small research team, support fieldwork, and help students move from data collection to final presentations.

Research experience

Team Member

For high school students interested in environmental science, earth science, and hands-on research at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory.

  • Applicants should be based in or able to commute to the NYC and Hudson Valley area.

Program Format

  • Six to eight week summer research experience.
  • Fieldwork, lab analysis, and data interpretation.
  • Small teams mentored by Columbia or LDEO researchers.
  • Final presentation to researchers and faculty.
