Team Lead Details

Guide a summer research team.

Team Leads help students organize fieldwork, manage research tasks, and keep students on track for final presentations alongside Columbia mentors.

  • Must be an undergraduate or graduate student.
  • All majors are encouraged to apply!
  • Strong communication and organization skills recommended.
  • Reliable transportation to Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory or Columbia University. Participants are not eligible for university housing.
  • Participants are expected to commit to the full 6 weeks of the summer program.
  • Time Commitment: 30-50 hours per week, depending on project scope. Up to 5 online preperation meetings before the program start. 1 program orientation.
  • Minimum Age: 16 years old.
  • Compensation: $3100 one-time stipend. Lunch is provided daily. Prepaid OMNY cards will be provided for the NYC Subway upon request.
Apply as Team Lead

Team Member Details

Join a real research project.

Team Members work with a small group to collect samples, analyze data, and present scientific findings at the end of the summer.

  • Open to high school students in the New York City and Hudson Valley area.
  • Interest in science, the environment, or the natural world encouraged.
  • No prior research experience required.
  • Students should be available for the full six to eight week summer program.
