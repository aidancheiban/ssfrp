# Secondary School Field Research Program

Website for the Secondary School Field Research Program (SSFRP), a scientific research program based at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory.

## About the program

SSFRP is made up of high school students, undergraduates, graduates, and Columbia scientists. Work is split into research teams that each focus on one project. Every team has a scientist who mentors, an undergraduate or graduate student who leads and keeps things on schedule, and high school students who run the research day to day.

Projects center on environmental research in the Piermont Salt Marsh, the Lamont Sanctuary Forest, and New York City. One project to come out of the program measured Particulate Matter 2.5 in the NYC subway.

## Pages

- `index.html` — home
- `projects.html` — projects overview, with a year archive in `project_years/` (2023–2026)
- `about.html` — about the program
- `apply.html` — application info and portal

## Project structure

```
index.html, about.html, apply.html, projects.html
project_years/        per-year project pages
css/global.css        all styles
js/main.js            nav, scroll effects, hero map lens, growth rail
assests/              images and video
```

## Built with

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The hero map uses [Leaflet](https://leafletjs.com/) with Esri World Imagery tiles.
